About Crowned Reads

✦ ◆ ✦

Crowned Reads is a cozy corner of the internet built for readers who fall hard for fantasy worlds, slow-burn romance, and stories that feel like spells.

We believe every book deserves a place to live beyond the last page — a space where readers can share what broke them, what healed them, and which characters they'd follow into any realm.

From Caraval's velvet mysteries to the dragon-soaked skies of Fourth Wing, our reviews celebrate the genre that crowned itself on bookstagram, BookTok, and every bestseller list in between.

What We Cover

  • Young Adult Romantasy
  • New Adult Fantasy
  • Fairy Tale Retellings
  • Enemies-to-Lovers Sagas
  • Magical Academies & Court Intrigue
